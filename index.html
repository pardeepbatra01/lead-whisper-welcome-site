Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VoiceAI Pro

How quickly can I set up VoiceAI Pro for my business?

+

VoiceAI Pro can be set up in under 2 minutes. Simply sign up, choose your Canadian phone number, record a brief greeting, and you're ready to start receiving calls. Our team provides onboarding support if needed.

Is VoiceAI Pro compliant with Canadian privacy laws?

+

Yes, VoiceAI Pro is fully compliant with PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) and all provincial privacy regulations. We use bank-grade encryption and store all data on Canadian servers.

Can the AI handle complex customer inquiries?

+

Our AI is trained to handle a wide range of inquiries, from simple appointment booking to detailed product questions. For complex issues it can't resolve, it intelligently transfers calls to you or takes detailed messages.

What happens if I exceed my monthly call limit?

+

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan anytime, or we'll offer reasonable overage rates. We never cut off your service unexpectedly.

Does VoiceAI Pro integrate with my existing business tools?

+

Yes! We integrate with popular Canadian business tools including QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, and many more. Custom integrations are available for Enterprise plans.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

+

Absolutely! You can port your existing Canadian phone number to VoiceAI Pro, or we can provide call forwarding from your current number. The process is simple and we handle all the technical details.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

+

No contracts required! You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're confident you'll love VoiceAI Pro, which is why we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
